About Us

We're helping apprentice electricians conquer theoritical subjects and concepts during trade school that are required for their licencing exams, capstone subjects and ultimately, their licenced career!

We've created an engaging, technology-driven and relevant electrical learning platform with videos, figures, articles and materials that make the learning process entirely online and interactive (interactive figures, tables and GBL software). This is the modern textbook for vocational and trade educational providers in Australia and New Zealand

These are the ed-tech companies we want to emulate:

  1. Brilliant
  2. Edrolo
  3. Engineering Mindset
  4. Khan Academy
  5. Immersive Linear Algebra

These are the features we want our initial platform MVP to launch with:

  • Fully interactive figures and tables that allow electrical variables to be manipulated
  • Built-in calculator that is easy to read and use that allows quick working out
  • Checkpoint, review and exam questions with solutions, with immediate feedback for wrong/right answers
  • Illustrative diagrams that guide students through questions
